Platform Features

Built for Precision

01

Live Client Dashboards

A dedicated portal for every client showing real-time operation status, cargo movements, inspection records, and SITREP summaries — updated continuously throughout the operation.

02

Real-Time SITREP Reports

Automated situation reports every 4 hours — tonnage handled, equipment performance, weather impact, workforce status, and key observations from our on-site team.

03

GPS & Time-Stamped Photos

Every photo captured by our inspectors is automatically tagged with GPS coordinates and a precise timestamp — creating an irrefutable, court-admissible evidence chain for every cargo event.

04

Mobile Inspection App

Our proprietary mobile app enables surveyors to capture damage assessments, serial numbers, condition reports, and digital checklists in the field — syncing instantly to the cloud.

05

Automated EDI Integration

Direct electronic data interchange with shipping lines, terminals, and logistics platforms. Cargo status updates pushed automatically — eliminating manual data entry and reducing errors.

06

Digital Checklists & Audit Trails

Every operation follows structured digital checklists with mandatory sign-offs at each stage. Full audit trails maintained — giving you complete traceability from first inspection to final delivery.
