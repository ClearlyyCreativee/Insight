Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 30 March 2026

Insight Cargo Solutions ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website insightcargosolutions.com (the "Website"). We are based in South Africa and take the protection of personal information seriously in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA).

1. Information We Collect

We collect zero personal information from visitors to our Website.

The Website is purely for visibility and informational purposes. There are no contact forms, newsletters, accounts, downloads, or any other features that require you to submit personal details. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics tools, or any other technology that collects or stores identifiable information about you.

2. Server Logs and Technical Data

Like most websites, our hosting server may automatically record basic technical information in temporary server logs (such as IP address, browser type, operating system, and pages visited). This data is used only for system administration, troubleshooting, and security purposes. It is not linked to any individual and is not stored long-term or shared with any third parties.

3. Purpose of Processing

Because we collect no personal information, there is no processing of personal information for marketing, profiling, sharing, or any other purpose.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose any personal information to third parties because none is collected.

5. Your Rights under POPIA

Since we do not collect or hold any personal information about you, there is nothing for us to provide, correct, or delete upon request.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised accordingly.
